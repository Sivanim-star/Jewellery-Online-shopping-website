How to Measure Your Ring Size

  • Wrap a string or paper strip around your finger base
  • Mark where it overlaps

  • For best fit, measure at the end of the day when fingers are largest

Jewellery Care & Investment

Expert guidance for preserving and growing your jewellery collection

💎

Care Tips

  • Store jewellery in a cool, dry place away from sunlight
  • Clean gold with mild soap and warm water
  • Use soft cloth for silver to prevent tarnishing
  • Keep diamonds separate to avoid scratches
  • Remove jewellery before swimming or exercising
  • Professional cleaning annually recommended
📈

Investment Value

  • Gold maintains value during economic uncertainty
  • Diamonds appreciate with rarity and quality
  • Platinum is rarer than gold, offering long-term value
  • Vintage and antique pieces often appreciate
  • BIS hallmark ensures resale value
  • Diversify with different metals and stones
✨

Authentication

  • Look for BIS hallmark on gold jewellery
  • Check for 925 stamp on sterling silver
  • Diamond certification from GIA or IGI
  • Verify seller credentials and reviews
  • Request original purchase receipts
  • Get appraisal from certified gemologist
🔒

Insurance & Safety

  • Insure valuable pieces separately
  • Keep detailed records and photographs
  • Use bank lockers for high-value items
  • Update appraisals every 2-3 years
  • Consider home safe for daily wear pieces
  • Register jewellery with insurance company
